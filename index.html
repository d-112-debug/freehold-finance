Freehold Finance

Freehold Finance is an Australian mortgage broking firm founded by Anup Shrestha, dedicated to helping everyday Australians turn their property dreams into reality. Our name says it all — houses, land, and property are tangible assets, and we provide the finance to help you own them. Based across Australia, we serve clients nationwide with a personalised, end-to-end home loan service that takes the complexity out of borrowing.

Navy text on white
Body content, cards, forms
Correct
Never place gold text on white — contrast is too low for accessibility. Gold is exclusively used on navy backgrounds or as a background colour itself.
Minimum contrast: All text must meet WCAG AA contrast ratio of 4.5:1 for body text. Navy on white (#1A1F71 on #FFFFFF) achieves 12.5:1 — well above requirement.
Avoid: Yellow/gold text on white backgrounds, navy on dark backgrounds without sufficient contrast, or introducing off-brand colours.

04 — Logomark

The logomark

The logomark is a standalone icon — used as a favicon, app icon, avatar, and in contexts where space is limited. It must work without the wordmark at small sizes.

Designer brief: A logomark is mostly composed of a single symbol or picture. A great medium for communicating your brand identity is a picture. The logomark’s chosen image’s color and form can both serve to highlight the brand’s individuality. Please use the information about the company to make a creative design. Simple, minimalistic but creative and meaningful. The icon below is for demo purpose only.

Logomark specification
ConceptGeometric house silhouette with a rectangular door cutout in brand gold. The door represents the threshold to ownership — the core promise of Freehold Finance.
ShapePentagon (house) — flat roof line, symmetrical, no decorative detail. Clean geometric form that scales to 16px without losing legibility.
Door elementCentred rectangle inset at base of house shape, sized approximately 35% of house width. Filled with brand gold #F7B600. Represents entry, access and ownership.
Primary coloursHouse: #1A1F71 (deep navy) · Door: #F7B600 (title gold)
Reversed versionHouse: #FFFFFF · Door: #F7B600 · Background: #1A1F71
Minimum size16px × 16px (favicon). Below this size, use initials "FF" mark instead.
Clear spaceMinimum clear space = 50% of the icon height on all sides. Never crowd the logomark with other elements.
File formats requiredSVG (primary), PNG @1x @2x @3x, ICO (favicon), PDF (print)
Designer noteThe placeholder SVG in this guide is a rough directional sketch only. Final logomark must be professionally drawn with optical corrections, refined proportions and vector precision.
05 — Wordmark

The wordmark

The wordmark combines the brand name in a typographic lockup. It is used alongside the logomark in horizontal or stacked arrangements, and standalone where the icon is not appropriate.

Designer brief: A wordmark is a brand name with a distinctive design. The precise typeface, color, and spacing of letters. The color and form can both serve to highlight the brand’s individuality.

06 — Tagline & Brand Voice

Tagline & brand voice

How Freehold Finance speaks — the words we use, the tone we strike, and the feeling we leave clients with.

Own it. For real.
The official brand tagline
Tagline rationale: "Own it. For real." plays on the legal meaning of freehold — full, unconditional property ownership. It is punchy, confident and direct. "For real" adds an authenticity edge, signalling that Freehold Finance cuts through jargon and delivers genuine outcomes. Use it on all primary brand materials including business cards, homepage hero, email signatures and marketing campaigns.
Brand voice pillars
Clear & direct
We say what we mean. No jargon, no fine print buried in complexity. Clients should walk away understanding exactly what they're getting.
"We compared 40 lenders and found you a rate 0.6% lower than your current one."
Plain language Specific numbers Banking jargon
Confident & grounded
We are experts at what we do. We speak with authority — not arrogance. We back our advice with evidence and stand behind our recommendations.
"This is the right loan structure for your situation — here's exactly why."
Assertive Evidence-based Wishy-washy
Human & approachable
Buying property is one of the biggest decisions of someone's life. We treat every client like a real person, not a transaction number.
"Buying your first home is exciting — and a little overwhelming. That's completely normal. Let's break it down together."
Empathetic Personal Corporate-cold
Tone of voice — do & don't
Write like this
  • "We work for you — not the bank."
  • "Get approved in as little as 24 hours."
  • "We'll find your lowest rate, guaranteed."
  • "No broker fees. Ever."
  • "Own it. For real."
Avoid this
  • "Pursuant to our assessment framework..."
  • "Leverage our synergistic lender panel..."
  • "We facilitate optimal debt structuring..."
  • "Subject to credit approval and eligibility..."
  • Excessive exclamation marks!!!
